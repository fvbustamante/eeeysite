Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my free trial or subscription?

You can cancel anytime from your iPhone or iPad settings:

  1. Open Settings on your device.
  2. Tap your name at the top.
  3. Select Subscriptions.
  4. Choose the subscription you want to manage.
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription and confirm.

Note: To avoid renewal, cancel at least 24 hours before the current period ends.

  • How do I restore a purchase? Open the app > Settings > Restore Purchases.
  • Forgot password? Tap “Forgot?” on the login screen. An email will guide you through reset.
  • App crashes at launch? Make sure you're on the latest version from the App Store. If the problem persists, contact us with device model + iOS version.
